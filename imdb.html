IMDb Sentiment ML Model Selection

This is the project from my class to predict a review is positive or negative based on dataset from IMDb. I am going to show the process that how we select the best binomial logistic regression model among Ridge, Lasso and Elastic-net.

Dataset summary

We have a dataset of 25,000 movies reviews from IMDB, labeled by sentiment (positive/negative). Reviews have been preprocessed, and each review is encoded as a sequence of word indexes (integers). For convenience, words are indexed by overall frequency in the dataset, so that for instance the integer "3" encodes the 3rd most frequent word in the data. This allows for quick filtering operations such as: "only consider the top 10,000 most common words, but eliminate the top 20 most common words".
